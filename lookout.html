Product Overview
Lookout is the primary dashboard component for Sentinel -- a larger effort for communities to better monitor and act on infectious disease outbreaks. Colorado Mesa University is Sentinel's first test site. Students use Scout to report symptoms and check into events while administrators use Lookout to access this data, which in turn informs real-time policies and safety measures on campus. While Lookout consists of many views, my focus was on the design and development of spatial visualizations.
Creating the Case Map
We first decided to build an interactive map to visualize the spatial distribution of the university's positive cases. I used Google's geocoding API to convert addresses to coordinates and D3's embedded geographic functionality to draw the basemap. For administrators, they were most interested in finding symptomatic students in locations with large numbers of positive cases, and being able to see these students' information in more detail.
