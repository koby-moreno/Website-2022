Hey, I'm Koby
I create digital experiences that allow us to better understand and navigate our physical world. As a Product Designer at Foursquare, I work on our mapping and data visualization platforms.
I've lived former lives as an architect, urban designer, GIS specialist, and front-end engineer and have collaborated on a range of projects including climate resilience planning at AECOM, infectious disease control at The Broad Institute, and in-hospital technologies at Mayo Clinic. I recevied my Masters from Harvard University's Graduate School of Design and a BS in Architecture from Washington University in St. Louis.
